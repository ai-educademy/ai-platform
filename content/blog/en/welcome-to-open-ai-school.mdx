## We're Live! 🎉

Today, we are thrilled to announce the official launch of **Open AI School** — a completely free, open-source platform dedicated to making AI education accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Whether you are a student, a professional looking to upskill, or simply curious about what Artificial Intelligence really is, this platform was built for you.

---

## Why Open AI School?

AI is transforming every industry — from healthcare and finance to entertainment and agriculture. Yet most AI education resources are either too expensive, too technical, or only available in English.

We believe that **understanding AI should not be a privilege**. It should be a fundamental literacy — like reading and writing — available to every person regardless of their background, language, or budget.

That is exactly why we created Open AI School.

---

## What's Available Right Now

Here is what you can explore today:

### 📚 10 Structured Programs

We have designed **10 comprehensive learning programs** organised into two clear tracks:

- **🌳 AI Learning Path** — Start from absolute zero and build your way up to understanding how AI works, from basics to real-world applications.
- **🔨 Craft Engineering Path** — Learn the engineering skills behind AI: clean code, system design, and building production-ready AI applications.

Each program has carefully crafted lessons that build on each other, so you always know what to learn next.

### 🎯 2 Learning Tracks

Whether you are more interested in **understanding AI concepts** or **building AI systems**, there is a track for you. Start with whichever feels right — you can always explore both.

### 🌍 5 Languages

All content is available in **English, French, Dutch, Hindi, and Telugu**. We are committed to breaking the language barrier in AI education. More languages are on the way.

### 🧪 Interactive Playground

Our **AI Playground** lets you experiment with AI models directly in your browser. No installation, no setup — just open it and start experimenting. Train a model, see it learn, and understand the concepts hands-on.

---

## Our Mission

Open AI School exists to **democratise AI education globally**. We are guided by three core principles:

1. **Free Forever** — No subscriptions, no paywalls, no "premium tier". Every lesson, every tool, every resource is completely free.

2. **Beginner-First** — We do not assume you know programming, mathematics, or anything technical. Every concept is explained using real-world examples you already understand.

3. **Multilingual by Design** — Education in your own language hits differently. We build content in multiple languages from day one, not as an afterthought.

---

## Built in the Open

Open AI School is **100% open source**. Every lesson, every line of code, every design decision is transparent and available on GitHub.

We welcome contributions from everyone:

- **Translate** lessons into your language
- **Write** new lessons or improve existing ones
- **Report** bugs or suggest features
- **Share** the platform with someone who might benefit

This is not just a platform — it is a community project. The more people contribute, the better it gets for everyone.
